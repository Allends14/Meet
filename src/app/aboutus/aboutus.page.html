About us Mission

Hair mo! Mission is to have a positive impact on our beneficiaries, both through the provision of wigs and through research.We want to reflect this positivity in everything we say and do so that we can have a positive impact on everyone we meet. We prefer to see opportunities rather than problems and offer simple and straightforward solutions. Hair Mo! are motivated by a desire to improve the lives of children and adolescents with cancer and to make our service available to all beneficiaries in need.

Vision

The vision of this project is to empower more benefactors and make each and every cancer patient intellect to moving forward the quality of life of children with cancer and their families through upgrading their passionate, social and restorative well-being.
